About Phantom Hacks

India's Hackathon Engine 👻

We aggregate hackathons from 20+ platforms so you never miss a chance to build, compete, and win. Launched in 2024 by a team of hackers for hackers.

📡

Real-Time Aggregation

We pull listings from Unstop, Devpost, Devfolio, HackerEarth, MLH, and 15+ more sources, refreshed every 2 minutes automatically.

🔍

Smart Discovery

Filter by skill level, team size, prize pool, mode, location, deadline, and tags. Find exactly the hackathon that fits your goals.

🏆

₹8.4Cr+ in Prizes

We track over ₹8.4 Crore in combined prize pools across 2,800+ active and upcoming hackathons worldwide — in India and globally.

🌐

India-First Focus

Special emphasis on Indian college fests, IIT/NIT events, government hackathons, and regional competitions across all 28 states.

🤝

Team Finder

Looking for teammates or want to join a team? Our Discord community connects 50,000+ hackers across India and beyond.

📰

Newsletter

Weekly digest of the hottest hackathons delivered to your inbox every Monday. Subscribe free — no spam, unsubscribe anytime.
